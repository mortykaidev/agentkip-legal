Status: **Canonical drafts - publish only after each described control is verified**

These guides explain the practical boundary between AgentKip, a self-hosted
Noggin, connected providers, and systems the user controls. They supplement the
Terms; they do not hide material risks or transfer AgentKip's own legal duties to
the user.

## Core rule

You remain responsible for deciding what systems AgentKip may access and for
reviewing actions you approve or deliberately enable. AgentKip remains responsible
for its own product behavior, privacy promises, security controls, and truthful
descriptions. A warning or checkbox does not replace engineering safeguards.

AgentKip is not designed for emergency, safety-critical, medical, legal,
financial, or other professional decision-making. AI output can be wrong,
incomplete, insecure, or manipulated by untrusted content.
