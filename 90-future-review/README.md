# Future Review Register

Status: **Internal**

The baseline is designed for an internally reviewed United States, adults-only,
self-hosted beta. It is not represented as attorney-reviewed. Day-one publication
does not require an attorney approval gate under the approved launch plan.

That decision does not make future legal risk static. Use this directory to stop
scope expansion long enough to update product controls, public documents, Apple
answers, provider records, and any specialized review appropriate to the change.

## Contents

- [Legal and compliance review triggers](LEGAL-REVIEW-TRIGGERS.md)
- [Unresolved questions](UNRESOLVED-QUESTIONS.md)

## Rule

A triggered review is an owner decision gate, not an automatic claim that outside
counsel is mandatory. The default recommendation is qualified review before
shipping minors, regulated data, enterprise obligations, UK/EEA distribution,
materially broader autonomous authority, or a response to a serious dispute or
regulatory event.
