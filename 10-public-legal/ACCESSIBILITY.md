# AgentKip Accessibility

**Updated:** July 15, 2026

**Version:** 2026.07.15-draft.1

Hayward Imagination Company LLC wants AgentKip’s app, website, legal information, and support
materials to be usable by people with disabilities. AgentKip is a beta, and this statement does not
claim certification or complete conformance with a particular accessibility standard.

## Our approach

We aim to:

- support iOS accessibility features such as VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, sufficient contrast, Reduce
  Motion, clear focus order, and accessible controls;
- keep legal and safety choices understandable, labeled, and available without relying only on
  color, motion, sound, or fine motor precision;
- make approval, denial, consent, revocation, export, and deletion flows operable with assistive
  technology;
- publish legal documents as structured text with descriptive headings and meaningful link labels;
- test the website with keyboard navigation, text enlargement, zoom/reflow, and common screen-reader
  patterns; and
- treat an accessibility problem that prevents informed consent or a safety decision as a release
  blocker for the affected flow.

## Known beta limitations

New or experimental screens may not yet work equally well with every assistive technology,
language, content-size setting, or input method. AI-generated content can also contain poor heading
structure, ambiguous link text, uncaptioned descriptions, or other inaccessible output. Review
generated material before publishing it.

We verify specific accessibility claims against the released build. This page should not be read as
a representation that AgentKip currently satisfies every criterion of WCAG, Section 508, or another
standard.

## Request help or report a barrier

Email hello@agentkip.ai with **“Accessibility”** in the subject. Please include the page or feature,
AgentKip version, device and iOS version, assistive technology, what you expected, and what happened.
Do not include passwords, tokens, private repository content, or unnecessary health information.

We will provide a reasonable alternative when practical and work to address verified barriers based
on impact and available beta resources. You may also request these legal documents in an alternative
accessible format.
