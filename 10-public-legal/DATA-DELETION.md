# AgentKip Data Deletion Notice

**Effective:** July 15, 2026

**Version:** 2026.07.15-draft.1

**Applies to:** United States adult beta users

This notice explains how to delete an AgentKip account and how that differs from disconnecting a
service or deleting information stored elsewhere. It supplements the [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

## 1. Delete your AgentKip account

Use **Settings → Legal & Privacy → Delete AgentKip Account** in the app. If you cannot access the
app, email hello@agentkip.ai from the address associated with your account.

We may verify your identity and authority before acting. The app or our response will show the
request as **requested**, **pending**, **completed**, **partially completed**, or **failed**. If a
category cannot be deleted, we will identify the category and explain why when legally permitted.

Account deletion is intended to remove or de-identify account data controlled by Hayward that is no
longer needed to provide the Service. It may be irreversible. Export information you need first.

## 2. What account deletion does not delete

Deleting an AgentKip account does not itself delete or undo:

- GitHub repositories, organizations, issues, pull requests, commits, branches, deployments, or
  authorization records controlled by GitHub;
- files, settings, credentials, backups, messages, applications, or other content on your phone,
  computer, server, or another device;
- data, logs, checkpoints, models, or configuration in a self-hosted Noggin instance;
- prompts, output, logs, or account data held independently by an AI provider;
- Apple account, App Store, iCloud, notification, purchase, or device records;
- Stripe payment or transaction records held under Stripe’s obligations;
- an action already completed in a Connected System; or
- records another person or organization controls.

Use each system’s deletion and revocation controls separately. Hayward cannot delete data from a
system it does not control.

## 3. Disconnecting and revoking integrations

Disconnecting stops future authorized use through that connection but does not delete prior data or
undo prior actions. To fully revoke access:

1. Disconnect the integration in AgentKip.
2. Revoke the app, installation, token, session, or credential in the provider’s settings.
3. Remove mirrored credentials from the configured host where applicable.
4. Rotate any credential that may remain exposed or shared.
5. Review the provider’s stored data and delete it there if desired.

Deleting the app from your phone does not necessarily delete your AgentKip account or revoke a
third-party authorization.

## 4. Local and self-hosted data

Use in-app clearing controls for eligible local history, cached data, diagnostic material, and
consent exports. iOS backups or synchronized copies may persist according to Apple and your device
settings.

For a self-hosted Noggin instance, the host owner controls its databases, logs, backups,
checkpoints, and deletion tools. Follow that installation’s documented procedure. If another person
or organization operates the host, send the request to that operator.

## 5. Information we may retain

We may retain a limited record when reasonably necessary to:

- document Terms acceptance, consent, authorization, transactions, deletion, or dispute history;
- meet tax, accounting, fraud-prevention, security, or other legal duties;
- detect repeated abuse or protect the Service and users;
- establish, exercise, or defend legal claims; or
- maintain a time-limited backup that is isolated from ordinary use and overwritten on its normal
  schedule.

Retained information is restricted to those purposes and deleted or de-identified when the reason
ends. We will not keep User Content merely because it might be useful in the future.

## 6. Timing

We will acknowledge and complete a verified request within the period required by applicable law.
Complexity, security investigation, legal holds, provider dependencies, or verification may affect
timing. If so, we will provide status and any extension or denial explanation required by law.

## 7. Other privacy requests

You may request access, correction, or a portable copy of personal information Hayward controls by
using available in-app controls or emailing hello@agentkip.ai. State law may provide additional
rights, exceptions, identity-verification requirements, or an appeal process.
