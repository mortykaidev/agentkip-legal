# New Zealand Activation Checklist

Last source check: 2026-07-15

## Legal and privacy assessment

- [ ] Confirm whether and how the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 applies to the
      proposed operations, providers, and users.
- [ ] Appoint and publish an appropriate privacy officer/contact process.
- [ ] Map every active data flow to the information privacy principles, including
      collection notice, purpose limitation, access, correction, security,
      retention, use, and disclosure.
- [ ] Assess every overseas disclosure under Information Privacy Principle 12;
      document comparable safeguards, contractual controls, applicability, or
      properly informed authorization as relevant.
- [ ] Verify data-access and correction workflows, identity verification,
      response handling, and complaint escalation.
- [ ] Add New Zealand serious-harm breach assessment and notification procedures
      to the incident playbook.
- [ ] Evaluate whether any AI, support, diagnostics, repository, voice, photo, or
      free-form content can contain sensitive or third-party personal information.

## Consumer and product assessment

- [ ] Review the Fair Trading Act and Consumer Guarantees Act implications for
      beta, warranty, liability, AI-accuracy, availability, and refund language.
- [ ] Ensure Michigan forum/law language does not purport to remove mandatory New
      Zealand consumer rights.
- [ ] Verify marketing, AI-capability, privacy, security, and self-hosting claims
      are substantiated for the New Zealand release build and configuration.
- [ ] Assess electronic-message, cookie/storage, and direct-marketing rules for
      the website and support communications.
- [ ] Verify pricing, taxes, currency, subscriptions, cancellation, and refund
      behavior if paid service is enabled.
- [ ] Confirm the 18+ positioning, App Store age rating, Terms, and onboarding
      remain consistent.

## Operational and publication gates

- [ ] Verify account deletion, export, correction, consent revocation, provider
      controls, and incident intake work for New Zealand users.
- [ ] Publish an approved New Zealand supplement only if the global documents do

- [ ] Use a non-residential business contact solution for any required public
      address.
- [ ] Update App Store availability, App Privacy answers, support routing, source

- [ ] Run localized link, accessibility, spelling, time-zone, date, and currency
      tests.
- [ ] Record Brandon's activation approval and evidence commit.
